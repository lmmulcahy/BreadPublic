# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

Bread ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Local Device Storage

Bread stores the following information locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

- **App Preferences**: Your timer settings for the BREAD method (Be Still, Encounter, Apply, and Devote timers)
- **Notification Settings**: Your preferred daily reminder time (if you choose to enable notifications)
- **App State**: Whether you have viewed the welcome screen

### No Personal Information Collected

Bread does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal information, including:
- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Your location or device identifiers
- Your reading history or progress
- Any analytics or usage data
- Any information that could identify you personally

## How We Use Information

All information stored by Bread is used solely to:
- Remember your timer preferences
- Schedule local notifications at your preferred time (if enabled)
- Maintain your app experience across sessions

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored **locally on your device** only
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app from your device

## Notifications

If you choose to enable notifications, Bread will:
- Request permission to send local notifications
- Schedule daily reminders at your selected time
- Store your notification preference locally on your device

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app. Notification permissions are managed entirely by iOS and can be revoked at any time.

## Third-Party Services

Bread may open external links to Bible Gateway (biblegateway.com) when you view scripture passages. When you visit these external websites, their own privacy policies apply. Bread does not transmit any information to these services.

## Children's Privacy

Bread does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have complete control over your data
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
- You can modify your preferences at any time within the app settings

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing or your preferred method of communication.

## Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with:
- Apple's App Store privacy requirements
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Other applicable privacy laws

---

**Summary**: Bread is a privacy-first application. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data remains on your device and is under your complete control.
